Cataclysm Changes

Rage Generation

Rage generation has been normalized in Cataclysm. Now when you take damage, you gain rage based on the amount of health lost compared to your overall health. Your damage output no longer affects rage generation. Critical strikes now generate double the rage, effectively bringing up the floor of warrior damage and allowing for better scaling.

Ability Changes

Battle and Commanding Shout

Now share a 1 minute cooldown. Give Strength and Agility or Stamina respectively to all people within 45 yards. Generate 20 rage.

Bladestorm

Improved to hit for 150% weapon damage (up from 100%) on all targets within range every 1 second for 6 seconds.

Heroic Strike and Cleave

Now share a 3 second cooldown (1.5 seconds during Inner Rage). Hit instantly instead of "queueing" as they used to.

Recklessness

Now a 5 minute cooldown. Grants 50% critical strike rating on all special attacks for 12 seconds.

Rend

Significantly improved and vital for your damage profile. Scales dynamically with your attack power.

Slam

Can be used while moving. Faster cast time (modified by haste, base cast time is 0.5 seconds).

Whirlwind

Now uncapped on targets but with reduced damage. Cooldown reduced by 6 seconds if it hits more than 4 targets.

Stance Requirement Removals

Stance requirements have been removed from key abilities:

  • Pummel
  • Shield Wall
