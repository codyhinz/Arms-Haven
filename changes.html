Rage generation

Previously in Wrath of the Lich King, rage generation was as simple as do or take more damage and gain more rage in return. Rage has been normalized overall in cataclysm. Now when you take damage you gain rage based on the amount of health lost compared to your overall health. Your damage output has no effect on rage generation now. Also if you critical strike you will generate double the rage. This effectively brings up the floor of warrior damage, and allows us to scale very well as we are famous for.

Ability Changes

Battle and Commanding Shout

Now both share a 1 minute cooldown and now give Strength and Agility or Stamina respectively to all people within 45 yards. These abilites also generate 20 rage.

Bladestorm

Super improved from WOTLK, now hits for 150% weapon damage(up from 100%) on all targets within range every 1 second for 6 seconds.

Heroic Strike and Cleave

These abilities now share a 3 second cooldown by default(during Inner Rage these abilites now have a 1.5 second cooldown). However with the great sundering there are no longer auto-attack modifier abilites. So these abilities now hit instantly instead of "queueing" as they used to.

Recklessness

Now a 5 minute cooldown, but one of the better iterations of this ability. You now gain 50% critical strike rating on all special attacks(yellow hits) for 12 seconds.

Rend

Most improved, this ability is actually used, and vital for your damage profile now. Scales dynamically like most debuffs in cataclysm(meaning your attack power increasing will increase its damage, so no snapshotting).

Slam

This ability now can be used while moving and is a much faster cast time(modified by haste, base cast time is 0.5 seconds).

Whirlwind

Pretty big improvement from wrath that it is now uncapped on targets however the damage is pretty toned down by comparison to previous iterations. If it hits more than 4 targets it's cooldown will be reduced by 6 seconds. (10 seconds down to 4 seconds)

Stance Requirements have been removed from a few key abilites:

Pummel and Shield Wall
